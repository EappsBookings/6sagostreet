High Foot Traffic

The Location

The Heart of Chinatown

Chinatown is Singapore's most culturally distinct precinct. Sago Street, once famous for its cake shops and history, has evolved into a vibrant commercial hub.

  • Connectivity: 3-minute walk to Chinatown MRT (NE4/DT19) and upcoming Maxwell MRT (TE18).
  • Dining: Surrounded by Michelin-recommended hawker stalls at Maxwell Food Centre and trendy cafes along Keong Saik Road.
  • Culture: Adjacent to the majestic Buddha Tooth Relic Temple & Museum.
  • Business: A stone's throw from the Central Business District (CBD), making it ideal for offices or retail.
