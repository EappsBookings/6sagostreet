The Location 地理位置

The Heart of Chinatown 牛车水中心

Chinatown is Singapore's most culturally distinct precinct. Sago Street, once famous for its cake shops and history, has evolved into a vibrant commercial hub. 牛车水是新加坡最具文化特色的街区。硕莪街曾以糕饼店和历史闻名，现已发展成为充满活力的商业中心。

  • Connectivity: 交通便利： 3-minute walk to Chinatown MRT (NE4/DT19) and Maxwell MRT (TE18). 步行 3 分钟即可到达牛车水地铁站 (NE4/DT19) 和麦士威地铁站 (TE18)。
  • Dining: 餐饮美食： Surrounded by Michelin-recommended hawker stalls at Maxwell Food Centre and trendy cafes along Keong Saik Road. 麦士威熟食中心的米其林推荐小贩摊位和恭锡路沿线的时尚咖啡馆环绕。
  • Culture: 文化底蕴： Adjacent to the majestic Buddha Tooth Relic Temple & Museum. 毗邻宏伟的佛牙寺龙华院。
  • Business: 商业中心： A stone's throw from the Central Business District (CBD), making it ideal for offices or retail. 距中央商务区 (CBD) 仅一箭之遥，是办公或零售的理想选择。
